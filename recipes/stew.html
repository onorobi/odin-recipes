Stew recipe

Let's try on of the most popular dish in Hungary :)!

Ingredients

  • Chicken thigh and breast
  • Onion
  • Hungarian paprika
  • Salt and Pepper

How to make

  1. Cut the onion to small pieces
  2. Put in hot fat or oil
  3. Put the meat on the onion
  4. Put some water on it
  5. Cook until it is ready, enjoy:)!
